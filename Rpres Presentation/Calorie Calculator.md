Body Mass Index
========================================================

With this calculator you can see your BMI and your weight status depending on that.
Your BMI is equal to $kg/m^2$ .

BMI Example


```
Height(CM): 180
```

```
Weight(KG): 72
```

```
BMI: 22
```

```
Weight Status: Normal
```
***

|Category          |BMI Range - kg/(m^2) |

|Normal            |18.5-25              |

|Obese Class II    |35-40                |
|Obese Class III   |>40                  |
